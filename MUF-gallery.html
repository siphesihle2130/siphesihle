Business concept

In our group we came up with an idea of designing a taxi/minibus ticket booking website. Our aim was to provide seamless experience for travelers, eliminating the hassle of traditional booking methods. The name of our business concept is Afriride. Unlike other traveling apps/websites, Afriride focuses specifically on long distance travel needs. Our website offers a user-friendly platform for booking long-distance taxis. Users can easily navigate the app, search for available seats, and complete the booking process effortlessly.
