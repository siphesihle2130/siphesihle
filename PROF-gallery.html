Oral Presentation

The objective of this assessment was to test our theoretical understanding of the subject and our verbal communication skills through a professionally assembled presentation. Group sizes ranged from a minimum of 4 to a maximum of 6 members, and my group consisted of 5 members, including myself. Our chosen topic was 'Discuss the current impact of social media on critical thinking skills: How does social media contribute to the development and/or decline of critical thinking skills (what are the pros and cons)?' I was responsible for the conclusion, which included interpreting a graph. We used PowerPoint to create the presentation and relied on Google to research articles related to our topic and to source images for the slides.
